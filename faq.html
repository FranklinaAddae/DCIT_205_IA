How much does the application fee cost?

Can one apply with more than one set of results?

Yes. You can apply with up to 3 sets of results.

Can the application forms get finished before the deadline?

No. please the e-voucher is generated from the Banks' system and cannot get finished.

What should the background colour of the passport photograph be?

It should be white.

Can I visit my application document after I have finished filling the form
online? If so, till when?

Yes. Until the May/June WASSCE results are published, you can go back to your application
form as many times as possible, either to view or to make changes.

What do I do if I have difficulty logging in?

Try logging in after 8 p.m. or late at night when the number of people trying to log in will have reduced.

Is the application process for Distance Education the same as for
programmes on the main campus?

Yes, it is.

What are the general entry requirements for computer science admission?

Admission into our Computer Science program requires a minimum grade of A in Core Mathematics,
grade B in Elective Mathematics, and grades of at least C in other science courses.
